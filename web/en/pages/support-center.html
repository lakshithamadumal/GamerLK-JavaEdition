FAQs

  • How to buy a game?
    Browse our store, select your desired game, click "Buy Now", complete the payment process, and the game will be added to your library.
  • How to download after purchase?
    After purchase, go to your library, find the game, and click the download button. The game will download to your computer.
  • Payment method issues?
    We accept all major credit cards and digital payment methods. If you encounter issues, please try a different payment method or contact support.
  • Refund policy
    All sales are final. Due to the nature of digital products, we do not offer refunds after purchase. Please check system requirements before buying.
